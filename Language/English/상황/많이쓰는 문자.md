
# 회화

`This guy is salty all the time`

#### 모르는게 좋을꺼야
`You don't want to know`
`Maybe you don't want to know`

---
#### 내가 알게 머야 (It's not my business)
`Frankly speaking, I couldn't care less`
`I couldn't care less about that stupid test.`
`I could't care less about what he does in his free time. We aren't even friends.`
`Why are you telling me this? I couldn't care less`

---
#### 길이 막혀서 꼼짝도 못했어.

`We got stuck in traffic for several hours`
`We got stuck in traffic due to accident yesterday`

---
